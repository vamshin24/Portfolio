Get to know more about Vamshi

As a software and robotics engineer, I am on a mission to develop intelligent and autonomous systems that have a real impact. I am fascinated by the potential of ML and AI to enhance robotic capabilities and enable more intuitive human-robot interaction. My work focuses on developing impactful solutions at the intersection of software and robotics, with a particular interest in areas such as perception, motion planning and control, navigation, and manipulation.

Beyond my formal education and work, I actively engage with the developer community by participating in hackathons and other events, where I enjoy collaborating and exploring new technologies.
